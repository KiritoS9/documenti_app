Culling Lab

Privacy Policy

Culling Lab respects your privacy.

This application is designed to help photographers review and organize photos quickly using AI-assisted tools while keeping users fully in control of their workflow.

Data Collection

Culling Lab does not automatically collect personal data.

Your photos are processed locally on your Mac whenever possible and are not automatically uploaded or shared.

Photos & Media

  • Your images remain under your control.
  • Culling Lab does not sell or share your photos.
  • No automatic cloud backup is performed by the app.
  • The app only accesses files explicitly selected by the user.

AI Features

AI features are designed only to assist users during the photo culling process.

The application does not automatically choose or delete photos on behalf of the user.

Third-Party Services

Culling Lab may use trusted third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, or future cloud functionality.

Additional information will be updated in this policy if new services are introduced.
